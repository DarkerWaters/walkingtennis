Walking Tennis is waiting for you

Whether you are just starting out, wanting to improve, or ready to return to sport after a gap, Walking Tennis is for you.
Anyone can play anyone, regardless of fitness, injury, age or gender...
It's all about strategy, placement, spin and skill!

The biggest new development in walking sport

Walking Tennis has evolved from a growing demand for walking sports. Essentially it's a slower version of the game; it's tennis as you know and watch every summer on the BBC, but at a walking pace.

The Walking Tennis Academy has carefully designed the entire game so anyone can play, regardless of age or fitness level. Our technical founder (Peter Coniglio) has incorporated cutting-edge physical science to maximise the benefits of the sport for fitness and flexibility while minimising the possibility of injury.

From those who have dropped out of the sport they love due to injury, to those who believed they had hung up their tennis racket many years ago, it really is for everyone.

Feel the benefits!

Walking Tennis is ideal for everyone because we start gently and build up gradually, aiming to do a little more each session. Regular weight-bearing exercise helps keep bones strong by forcing the bones in the lower half of your body to support your full weight each time you move.

Anyone taking part in Walking Tennis will start seeing the benefits right from the start:

* less fat and more muscle
