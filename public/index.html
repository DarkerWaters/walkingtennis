Walking Tennis is waiting for you

Whether you are just starting out, wanting to improve, or ready to return to sport after a gap, Walking Tennis is for you.
Anyone can play anyone, regardless of fitness, injury, age or gender...
It's all about strategy, placement, spin and skill!

For the young

For kids, walking tennis is a perfect introduction to matches without all those pesky 'out' calls.
